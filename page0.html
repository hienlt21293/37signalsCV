Hi, my name is Hien

and this is my more-than-just-the-bare-minimum application for the 37signals SRE position.

As you can probably tell, this site’s design is heavily inspired by Omarchy.

Each “workspace” summarizes my experience at a company or on a project. You can navigate between them using the number keys (1, 2, 3, etc.) or the navigation bar at the top of the page.

The pop-up menu contains links to my resume and other materials, including a few fun projects I built specifically to hone my skills for this application. You can open it by clicking the menu icon in the top-left corner or pressing the backtick (`) or dot (.) key.

To return to this page, simply press W or the number 0.
